Natural Disasters

What is a Natural Disasters?

Natural Disasters are catastrophic events that result from any of the Earth’s natural phenomena. These can range from floods and hurricanes to tsunamis and earthquakes

Definition: A major event brought about by the natural processes of the Earth that causes widespread destruction to the environment and loss of life
